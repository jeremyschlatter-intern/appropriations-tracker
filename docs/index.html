About This Tool

The Appropriations Bill Version Tracker solves a real problem for congressional staff, advocates, and researchers: appropriations committees publish bill text and committee reports on their websites well before that information appears on Congress.gov.

This tool aggregates documents from multiple sources into a single view, making it easy to:

  • Find the latest version of any appropriations bill at any stage
  • Track which bills have moved through which stages
  • Access committee reports, amendments, and roll call votes
  • Compare bill versions across stages (subcommittee vs. full committee, House vs. Senate)
  • See the complete picture for the current and previous fiscal years

    Why This Matters

    The gap between when committee documents are published and when they appear on Congress.gov can be days or weeks. During active markup season, congressional staff, lobbyists, and advocacy organizations need immediate access to bill text and report language to do their jobs effectively. This tracker bridges that gap.
